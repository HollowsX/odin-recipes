Alex's Akatsuki Ramen Recipe


What You Need

  • 4 eggs
  • 6 cups chicken stock
  • 2 cups diced rotisserie chicken meat
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1 (1 inch) piece ginger, grated
  • 1 (12 ounce) package ramen noodles

    • Toppings


  • 1 cup finely crumbled dried seaweed, or to taste
  • 4 green onions, chopped, or to taste
  • 2 Thai chile peppers, stemmed and coarsely chopped, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce, or to taste

Directions

  1. STEP 1: Bring a small pot of water to a boil. Reduce heat to low and gently lower eggs into the water. Cook for 8 minutes. Remove eggs from hot water, cool under cold running water, and peel.
  2. STEP 2: Combine chicken stock, chicken meat, spinach, soy sauce, sriracha sauce, rice vinegar, garlic, and ginger in a large pot. Simmer until heated through, about 10 minutes.
  3. STEP 3: Meanwhile, bring a saucepan of water to a boil. Cook ramen noodles for 3 minutes. Drain.
  4. STEP 4: Ladle noodles into bowls and cover with soup. Add 1 soft-boiled egg to each bowl. Garnish soup with seaweed, green onions, chile peppers, and sriracha sauce.
  5. STEP 5: Turn on the TV and watch Naruto while eating
